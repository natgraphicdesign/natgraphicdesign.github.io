about

Natalia Ramírez González (San Juan, Puerto Rico, 1995) is a graphic designer. She received her BA in Art Theory from the University of Puerto Rico, and completed her postgraduate studies in Communications Graphic Design at TRACOR, in Madrid, Spain. Her interest in graphic design encompasses brand identity and UX/UI design. She currently resides and works in San Juan, Puerto Rico.
